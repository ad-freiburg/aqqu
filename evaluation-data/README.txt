These are various files used for evaluation. The json versions contain question
and answers. For Free917 also a gold SPARQL query is also provided.

free917.test.json: The test portion of Free917
free917.train.json: The train portion of Free917
free917.test_graphparser.json: The test portion of Free917 used by Graphparser (Reddy et al.)
webquestions.test.json: The test portion of Webquestions.
webquestions.train.json: The train portion of Webquestions.

The _1of2 files correspond to splits, sometimes 50/50 sometimes 20/80.
